 FARM WAR ALLIANCE



The Farm War Alliance was created to allow farming clans to participate in arranged wars with other farming clans that understand the farm war concept.

We are an International group of over 750+ proud clans and are adding a ton more every week.

We take turns winning and all have similar easy to 3-Star bases.

This allows our clans to gain considerable clan XP and loot.
